3、提升运算速度
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. code:: ipython3

    %timeit df.groupby("Gender").size()


.. parsed-literal::

    564 µs ± 10.8 µs per loop (mean ± std. dev. of 7 runs, 1000 loops each)


.. code:: ipython3

    %timeit df_cat.groupby("Gender").size()


.. parsed-literal::

    324 µs ± 5 µs per loop (mean ± std. dev. of 7 runs, 1000 loops each)
